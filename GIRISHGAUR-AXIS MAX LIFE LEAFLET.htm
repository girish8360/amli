AXIS MAX LIFE INSURANCE CO. LTD.

​Axis Max Life Insurance Limited, formerly known as Max Life Insurance Company Limited, is a prominent Indian life insurance company headquartered in New Delhi. It operates as a joint venture between Max Financial Services Limited and Axis Bank Limited, with Max Financial Services holding an 80% stake and Axis Bank holding 20%
Axis Max Life Insurance is a joint venture between:
  1. Max Financial Services Limited (MFSL): Holding an 80% stake, MFSL is part of the Max Group, an Indian multi-business corporation
  2. Axis Bank Limited: Holding a 20% stake, Axis Bank is one of India's largest private-sector banks
The collaboration between Max Life and Axis Bank evolved from a distribution partnership in 2010-11 to shared branding in 2024, reflecting a deepening strategic alliance

Key Highlights of AXIS MAX LIFE INSURANCE

  1. Established Presence:: Founded in 2001, Axis Max Life Insurance has over two decades of experience in the Indian insurance sector.​
  2. Comprehensive Product Portfolio:The company offers a wide range of insurance products, including term life insurance, unit-linked insurance plans (ULIPs), endowment policies, money-back policies, whole life insurance, retirement plans, and health insurance solutions.​

Smart Term Plan Plus (STPP)

​

The STPP Term Plan by Axis Max Life Insurance, officially known as the Smart Term Plan Plus (STPP), is a comprehensive term insurance solution designed to offer flexible and customizable protection to meet diverse financial needs.
STPP offers 7 distinct plan variants, allowing policyholders to choose coverage that aligns with their specific requirements
Regular Cover Provides a fixed sum assured throughout the policy term
FEATURES
  1. What it does: Simple level life cover— base Sum Assured remains the same throughout the policy term; on death the lump-sum Sum Assured is paid and policy terminates.
  2. Useful when: You want a traditional term plan with straightforward lump-sum protection
Rebalancing Cover Initially allocates 10% of the sum assured to life cover and 90% to accidental death benefit, with annual adjustments until the entire sum assured becomes life cover.​
FEATURES
  1. What it does: Splits the Base Sum Assured into a Life Cover SA and an Accidental Death Benefit (ADB) SA. At inception Life SA starts low (e.g., 10%) and ADB high (e.g., 90%); every policy anniversary Life SA increases by a fixed amount (10% of base SA simple interest) while ADB decreases by the same amount.
    — effectively rebalancing accidental vs non-accidental protection over time.
  2. Useful when: You want higher accidental cover initially (e.g., for younger working years) that progressively shifts toward regular life cover later
Early Return of Premium (ROP) Plus Returns 50% of total premiums paid at a specified milestone, with the remaining 50% returned at the end of the policy term, while maintaining life cover throughout.
FEATURES
  1. What it does: On survival to a specified milestone age (whichever is higher of attainment of age 60 or entry age + PPT + 10), the policy returns 50% of total premiums paid and continues with 50% of base sum assured for the remaining term; the remaining 50% of premiums is paid back at final maturity. (Full details depend on chosen PPT and policy term.)
  2. Useful when: You want a mix of term cover plus partial early recovery of premiums (helps those wanting mid-life liquidity or lower cover later).
Smart Cover Offers 150% of the base sum assured in case of death within the first 15 policy years, and 100% thereafter.
FEATURES
  1. What it does:Higher cover in early years — 150% of base Sum Assured is payable if death occurs within the first 15 policy years; thereafter 100% of base SA applies.
  2. Useful when: You need extra protection in the initial phase (e.g., while you have large liabilities such as a home loan or young dependents). Note: Regular pay may not be available in this variant (check PPT restrictions).
Return of Premium Refunds 100% of total premiums paid upon survival till the end of the policy term.
FEATURES
  1. What it does:If the Life Insured survives the policy term, 100% of total premiums paid (plus any underwriting extra and modal loadings) are returned at maturity.
    On death during the term the base Sum Assured is paid and policy terminates.
  2. Useful when:You prefer a term policy but want your premiums refunded if you outlive the cover (higher cost vs plain term
Whole Life Cover Provides coverage up to 100 years of age, with partial premium returns at specified milestones.
FEATURES
  1. What it does:Gives lifecycle options — on survival to a specified milestone (similar to Early ROP rules) the policy pays back 50% of premiums and continues life cover at 50% of base SA till the end of the policy; the remaining 50% of premiums are payable at age 100 when the policy then terminates.
    Death benefit rules and continued cover differ from conventional term plans — this variant provides life cover up to advanced ages. .
  2. Useful when: You want long-term/whole-life style protection (cover beyond standard term) plus return of part of premiums at milestones
Income Protection Cover Delivers monthly income to beneficiaries in the event of the policyholder's death, with options for level or inflation-adjusted payouts
FEATURES
  1. What it does:On death, instead of a single lump sum the nominee receives a monthly income (the chosen monthly income at inception) starting from the month after claim intimation and continuing up to the higher of 120 months or the outstanding term (months).
    You can choose Level Income or Inflation-proof Income (inflation proofing = +10% every 3 years, simple interest, capped at 200% of initial monthly income).
    Sum Assured on death for calculation is based on monthly income × specified multiple or outstanding months (see policy).
  2. Useful when: You want to replace the insured’s salary with steady monthly payments to dependents rather than giving a single lump sum

Also different variants have different minimum/maximum entry ages, PPT and policy term limits. Always verify variant-specific eligibility

    Annuity Variants available
    SUB-VARIANT
    Single Premium Variants - Immediate Annuity
    Single Life without Death Benefit This variant provides annuity for life till death of the annuitant. Death benefit is not applicable under this variant.
    Joint Life without Death Benefit: This variant provides annuity for life till death of last survivor. Death benefit is not applicable under this variant
    Single Life with Death Benefit This variant provides annuity for life till death of the annuitant with return of proportion of Total Premiums Paid on death. The proportion can vary from 25% to 100% (in multiples of 25%) and has to be chosen at inception only.
    Joint Life with Death Benefit This variant provides annuity for life till death of last survivor with return of proportion of Total Premiums Paid on first death and the remaining Total Premiums Paid shall be payable upon second death. The proportion can vary from 0% to 100% (in multiples of 25%) and has to be chosen at inception only. To be noted that in case of first death during the first five policy years, the annuity shall continue till death of the last survivor, thereafter, 100% of the Total Premiums Paid shall be payable upon second death & policy terminates.

    proportion of Annuity to Last Survivor

    Following sub-variants are available under this variant:
    Joint Life without Death Benefit A fixed annuity amount, guaranteed at policy inception, shall be payable in arrears till both the annuitants are alive. On first death of either of the annuitants, annuity payable to the last survivor shall be revised to the chosen annuity multiple times (either 50% or 110%) the annuity payable at inception.No death benefit shall be payable
    Joint Life with Death Benefit A fixed annuity amount, guaranteed at policy inception, shall be payable in arrears till both the annuitants are alive. On first death of either of the annuitants, annuity payable to the last survivor shall be revised to the chosen annuity multiple times (either 50% or 110%) the annuity payable at inception. On death of the last survivor, 100% of the Total Premiums Paid shall be payable and policy will terminate

    Immediate Annuity with
    Early Return of Premium (ROP)
    Single Life with Death Benefit This variant is available for Single Life with Death Benefit Only. In this variant, the policyholder has a flexibility to take early return of either 50% or 100% of Total Premiums Paid upon survival till the chosen milestone age and 100% of Total Premiums Paid less benefit already paid at the milestone age, if any, shall be payable to the nominee on death of the annuitant. The milestone age can be opted amongst 70/75/80/85 years of Age (last birthday). The proportion of Total Premiums Paid (i.e.50% or 100%) and milestone age has to be chosen at inception of the policy.

    Immediate Annuity for
    Guaranteed Period and Life thereafter
    Single Life without Death Benefit This option is available for Single Life without Death Benefit only. In this variant, policyholder can choose the guaranteed period of 5/10/15/20 years at inception of the policy. The annuity under the policy shall continue till the end of guaranteed period or till the death of the annuitant, whichever is later. On death of the annuitant after the guaranteed period (as opted at inception), the policy will terminate without any further benefit payout.

    Single Life Increasing Annuity Every 3 Years with Death Benefit : In this variant, the annuity at inception is guaranteed to increase @15% (Simple Increase) in every 3 years. Upon death of the annuitant, 100% of the Total Premiums Paid shall be payable and policy will terminate.

    Deferred Annuity

    In this variant, policyholder has to opt deferment period and premium payment term at the inception of the policy. The annuity payment starts after the end of deferment period based upon the chosen annuity payment frequency. The Deferment period should always be equal or greater than premium payment term. Following sub-variants are available under this variant:
    Single Life with Death Benefit till Deferment Period Single Premium: Under this variant, a fixed annuity amount, guaranteed at policy inception, shall be payable in arrears after the expiry of the deferment period till death of the annuitant. On death of the annuitant during deferment period, minimum 105% of the total premium, shall be payable to the nominee(s) as death benefit.
    Joint Life with Death Benefit till Deferment Period Single Premium Under this variant, a fixed annuity amount, guaranteed at policy inception, shall be payable in arrears after the expiry of the deferment period till death of the last annuitant. On death of the last annuitant during deferment period, minimum 105% of the total premium, shall be payable to the nominee(s) as death benefit.

    1. This is an option to withdraw present value of annuities payable in next 5 years in advance as a lump sum.
