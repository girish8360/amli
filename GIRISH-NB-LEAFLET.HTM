    ReAssure 3.0

    The Niva Bupa ReAssure 3.0 plan builds on this foundation, introducing a no-limits approach to coverage means UNLIMITED SUM INSURED. It offers unlimited base sum insured across all variants, immediate 145 pre-existing disease (PED) coverage options, and a rewards system that incentivises healthy living. With four variants—Classic, Select, Elite, and Black—it caters to diverse needs, from affordability to premium care.
    ReAssure 3.0 also integrates wellness-focused benefits and global treatment options, making it a holistic solution for modern healthcare challenges.


    KEY FEATURES

    1. UNLIMITED SUM INSURED : Unlike traditional health insurance plans with fixed coverage limits, ReAssure 3.0 provides unlimited base sum insured across all variants. This ensures that medical emergencies, whether minor or major, don’t leave you financially strained. Whether you’re facing a prolonged hospital stay or advanced treatment, this feature guarantees coverage without the fear of exhausting your benefits.
    2. Day 1 PED COVER :Pre-existing diseases usually come with long waiting periods, but ReAssure 3.0 changes that. By paying a small underwriting loading, you can get a waiver on waiting periods for up to 145 pre-existing conditions and enjoy coverage from day one. If you prefer, you can also choose the standard 36-month waiting period, giving you the flexibility to decide what works best for your health needs.
    3. CASH-BAG+ REWARDS :ReAssure 3.0 introduces a unique rewards system called Cash-Bag+. After the first claim-free year, you receive 10% of your renewal premium back, followed by 5% each subsequent year. Completing three consecutive claim-free years earns an additional 10% bonus. These rewards can be used for outpatient department (OPD) expenses, premiums, or co-payments and can even be shared with nominated individuals, making it a versatile financial tool.
    4. HEADSUP BENEFITS :Planning hospitalisation can reduce stress and costs. The HeadsUp feature guides you to the right hospital when you inform the insurer 24 hours in advance, ensuring cost-effective and efficient care. This is particularly useful for planned treatments, enhancing convenience and coordination.
    5. NIVABUPA ONE:ReAssure 3.0 goes beyond traditional insurance with NivaBupaOne, a premium service offering priority support, a dedicated relationship manager, and executive health assessments. This ensures personalised care and streamlined access to healthcare services, elevating the overall experience.
